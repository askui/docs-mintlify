## System Requirements

AskUI SDK is compatible with:

- **Windows**: Windows 10 or newer
- **macOS**: macOS 10.15 (Catalina) or newer
- **Linux**: Ubuntu 18.04 or newer

## AskUI Account

To use AskUI, you'll need to create an account:

1. Sign up at [askui.com](https://askui.com)
2. Activate your **free trial** (no credit card required)
3. Access your workspace with:
   - Installer downloads
   - Access tokens
   - Project templates

## Before You Begin

Ensure you've completed the installation process on your system before continuing to the next steps. This will guarantee a smooth setup experience for your first AI Vision Agent.
