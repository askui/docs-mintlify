---
title: AskUI-RemoveAgentOSService
---


### SYNOPSIS

Removes the AskUI AgentOS Service.

### NOTES

This Commandlet is only available on Windows.
This Commandlet must be executed in an elevated PowerShell session.

### EXAMPLES

#### EXAMPLE 1

Removes the AskUI AgentOS Service.

```powershell
Remove-AskUIAgentOSService
```
